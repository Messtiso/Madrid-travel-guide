Helpful Advice for Visitors

Planning ahead can help you make the most of your visit to Madrid. Here are a few useful tips for travellers.

  • Exchange currency at official exchange points for the best rates.
  • Try traditional Spanish cuisine such as tapas, tortilla española, and paella.
  • Learn a few basic Spanish phrases – locals appreciate the effort.
  • Be aware of pickpockets in busy tourist areas and on public transport.
  • Madrid has hot summers and mild winters, so pack clothing appropriate for the season.
  • Carry a reusable water bottle and stay hydrated during sightseeing.
  • Consider purchasing a public transport pass if you plan to travel frequently around the city.
