Getting Around the City

Madrid has a well-connected public transport system, making it easy for visitors to travel around the city.

Rail

Suburban and regional train services connecting Madrid with nearby areas.

Taxi

Readily available throughout the city and useful for short journeys.

Metro

A fast and reliable underground system with wide city coverage.

Bus

A large bus network connecting neighbourhoods and attractions.
